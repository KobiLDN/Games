## 240SX — A Drift Study

Top-down drift sim. Nissan S13 Coupé physics, tire smoke, skid marks, lap timing.

- [Circuit Edition](https://kobildn.github.io/Games/top%20down%20racing/240SX.html) — closed circuit with lap timing and checkpoints
- [Open Road](https://kobildn.github.io/Games/top%20down%20racing/240SX%20v1%20-%20open%20road.html) — free roam drift scoring

**Keyboard:** `W/↑` throttle · `A/D` steer · `Space` handbrake · `R` reset

**Mobile:** hold to accelerate · drag left/right to steer · drag down to brake · second finger for handbrake
